Pyramid FAQ

How do I join?

Submit declare, transfer the required USDT on the required network, then wait for confirmation.

When should I use claim-transfer?

Use claim-transfer when transfer metadata cannot be auto-bound to your declaration session.

What is TXID binding?

TXID binding links your declaration to a specific on-chain transfer record. It helps attribute identity for exchange or delayed settlement paths.

Common errors

  • Wrong chain or token.
  • Missing or invalid TXID.
  • Expired declaration session.
  • Trying to claim a transfer that does not match declared context.

Counterexample

Sending from an unrelated address and submitting another user's TXID will not prove ownership and can be rejected.
