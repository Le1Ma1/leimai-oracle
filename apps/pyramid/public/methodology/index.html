Pyramid Methodology

The Pyramid is a public clock-war game. Time is purchased and contested on-chain.

Core Rule

1 USDT = 86400 seconds of countdown value.

Gameplay

  • Step 1: Declare nickname + manifesto.
  • Step 2: Transfer USDT (TRC20) to the target wallet.
  • Step 3: If needed, use claim-transfer with TXID to bind identity.
  • Step 4: Higher effective contribution can usurp the throne.

Usurpation Threshold

The current Architect can be replaced when a challenger satisfies the engine usurpation condition based on on-chain credited value and active timer state.

Risk Notice

  • Wrong chain / wrong token may lead to irreversible loss.
  • Malformed declaration can prevent expected identity binding.
  • TXID replay or mismatched sender data can be rejected.
  • Do not send funds if network/token requirements are unclear.
